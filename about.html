Our Mission

At Najam Institute Foundation, our mission is to provide critical humanitarian aid and support to those affected by crises, particularly in Palestine. Since partnering with Muslims Around the World (MATW) on October 7, 2023, we have impacted over 51,532,844 lives through our charitable efforts, focusing on food security, clean water, medical aid, and resettlement support.

We are committed to restoring dignity and hope to vulnerable communities, especially orphans, displaced families, and those facing extreme hardship. Our work is guided by the principles of compassion, unity, and faith, ensuring that every donation makes a tangible difference.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual, regardless of circumstance, has access to basic necessities, safety, and opportunities to thrive. Our goal is to empower communities through sustainable aid, education, and psychological support, particularly for the 7,000+ orphans we serve in regions like Khan Younis, Deir Al-Balah, and Rafah Refugee Camp.

By fostering unity within the global Ummah, we aim to create lasting change, ensuring that no one is left behind in times of crisis.
